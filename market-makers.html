Introducing Koinpark Market Maker Program

Summary of Contents

  • Market Maker Program
  • Benefits for Market Makers

Greetings Tribe,

Koinpark is currently seeking Market Markers globally as we work to enhance our offerings for users and add more liquidity to our exchange. Top-tier liquidity providers are invited to participate in the Koinpark Market Maker Program.

You can join our Market Maker Program right now if your 30-day trading volumes are $5 million USDT or higher (or you can attain volumes of $5 million USDT). Reduced trading fees and additional incentives are available to market makers.

Market Maker Program

  • The program is only available for spot markets on Koinpark.com, and your score is based on a certain "Market Maker Pair List."
  • The Market Maker Pair List will be updated periodically by Koinpark.
  • Based on how well they performed across all of the pairs, market makers will receive a composite score.
  • According to the score rankings, market maker fees are assigned.

The composite score will be determined, among other things, by taking into account includes Maker Volumes and Weight Modification on Particular Pairs.

Advantages for Market Makers

  • Handling of support issues on a priority basis
  • Priority tech assistance
  • Reduced costs for Koinpark

If you're interested in applying, kindly complete this form or send an email with some documentation of your market-making volumes.
