Regulations for Law Enforcement/Legal Requests

Only For Government and Law Enforcement Entities

These regulations can be used by government and law enforcement organizations to submit information requests. In compliance with our Terms of Use and relevant regulations, Koinpark will evaluate each situation individually and work with you to release information as needed.

You must be a law enforcement officer or a government representative with the authority to make a law enforcement request.

1) Collect information to support an investigation, or

2) Submit an official law enforcement request.

These instructions are given to law enforcement officers or other government representatives who wish to access Koinpark data in order to gather information related to or helpful to an ongoing criminal investigation. Please be aware that these rules may occasionally change without prior notice. Private parties, whether involved in civil litigation or private investigations, are not the intended audience for these rules.

Mandatory for court orders and warrants

In accordance with the Koinpark Terms of Use and Privacy Policy, as updated from time to time, Koinpark collects, utilizes, and keeps user records. This comprises data gathered from customers in order to register a Koinpark account, offer them services, and comply with legal requirements (such as those relating to mandatory record keeping, anti-money laundering (AML), and know-your-client (KYC) duties). Please visit https://Koinpark.com/legal/privacy for more information.

Koinpark will need a legitimate court order from a competent jurisdiction or police orders/warrants in order to reveal information about our users in support of an ongoing criminal investigation. Please be aware that occasionally we may need extra details to confirm the legality of the information release.

Requests for Preservation Orders

Koinpark will take action to preserve relevant records for 90 days after receiving a request for a preservation order related to a criminal investigation. An extra request must be made if the preservation term has to be extended.

About Koinpark

The term "Koinpark" refers to an ecosystem that includes Koinpark platforms, websites, clients, and applets that are developed to provide Koinpark Services. Koinpark websites, whose domain names include but are not limited to http://www.Koinpark.com, mobile applications, clients, and other applications are also included in the ecosystem. As Koinpark continues to expand, it might eventually provide even more services.

Request Form

A request for information may be sent by authorized law enforcement organizations. Law enforcement officers are reminded to explicitly state the legal justification for each request for information or assistance in order to speed up the procedure. We can expedite the request by providing a summary of the relevant background information, the results of the inquiry, and any specifics pertaining to Koinpark users and transactional information. When submitting an information request, kindly use your business email address.

Request Notice

Before disclosing any personal data, we may notify the relevant user of the request, unless otherwise stated in any court order or police warrant and on a legitimate legal basis.

Please be aware that requests without copies of official supporting papers won't be handled.

We really appreciate your cooperation and consideration.
