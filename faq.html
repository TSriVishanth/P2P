1. Why is identity verification necessary?

Identity verification is used by Koinpark to adhere to KYC (Know Your Customer) standards. KYC is a technique for locating and verifying a customer's identity. It involves several steps and works to prevent the creation and use of fraudulent accounts.

2. What documents should I submit for KYC verification?

To complete your KYC, you must submit the following documents.

  • Verification of Tax ID - PAN Card (Mandatory for users of India)

  • National Identity document - A valid passport, driver's license, voter card, or Aadhaar card serve as national identification documents.

  • For international users - Do contact our support team so they can help you with the KYC verification procedure.

3. How do I complete my KYC?

Important: In order to continue using withdrawal services without interruption, all users must complete their KYC requirements as soon as possible.

To finish your KYC, follow the below steps

  • Download and open the Koinpark or Koinpark app on your mobile phone.

  • Complete your KYC there by going to ACCOUNT, Account Settings, and Account Details.

  • Submit after uploading all necessary documents.

  • Following the successful verification of your documents, you will receive an email confirmation.

Note: You do not need to complete the KYC verification process again if you are already a Koinpark user.

4. How long will it take me to complete my KYC process?

The KYC verification process by Koinpark takes between 24-48 hours to complete.

5. Can I use Koinpark to deposit or withdraw funds without completing my KYC?

For cryptocurrency : Users must comply with KYC requirements in order to use the deposit/withdrawal services.

For INR : Users must comply with KYC requirements in order to use INR deposit and withdrawal services.

6. Will Koinpark keep my KYC documents secure?

Security is our top priority and the most crucial component in Koinpark. We can promise you that we keep all of your information in strict confidence. Our identity management system and crypto-wallets both utilize the same security software.

1. What document must I provide to verify my bank account?

To complete your bank account verification, one of the following documents must be submitted.

  • Canceled cheque.

  • Front page of the passbook with a bank statement in PDF format

2. How do I complete the bank account verification process?

To complete your bank account verification, follow these steps:

  • Open the Koinpark app, then select ACCOUNT.

  • Select Add Bank Account under Account Settings after clicking.

  • Your bank account information (Account number and IFSC code) must be entered.

  • Verify the account information twice, then click Proceed.

  • Click Verify OTP and enter the OTP that was delivered to your registered mobile number.

  • As soon as your account has been added, you can begin to Add Funds..

3. Can I register more than one bank account with Koinpark?

We do not yet offer the possibility to add numerous bank accounts. Only one bank may be added to your Koinpark account.

6. How do I reset 2FA?

The procedures listed below can be used to switch your preference from OTP to SMS if you have 2FA enabled on your account:

  • Visit the Koinpark website and log in.

  • Select Disable under the Google Authenticator option

  • Please take note that in order to disable 2FA, you must first have access to it.

If you do not yet have access to 2FA, kindly open a support ticket and include a picture of yourself holding your PAN card. After that, a member of our support staff will reset your account's 2FA.

In order to log into your Koinpark account, follow these steps:

To change your Koinpark account password, follow these steps:
